Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how planesignal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit planesignal.com or get in touch with us about a brand sponsorship or creator partnership.

We keep things simple. We only collect what we need to do our job, we don't sell your data, and we don't track you around the internet.

What we collect

When you submit a form on this site (the "Get a quote", "Apply to join", or contact form), we collect the information you choose to share with us, which may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company, brand, or channel details
  • Budget range, audience size, niche, or any other context you provide
  • Any free-text notes you include in the message field

We do not collect this information automatically. You give it to us by filling in the form.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not use marketing cookies, advertising trackers, or third-party analytics tools. The only cookies that may be set are strictly necessary ones served by the platforms our forms run on.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Reply to your enquiry and discuss a potential partnership
  • Match brands with creators (or vice versa) where relevant
  • Keep records of campaigns, contracts, and payments we work on together
  • Send you occasional updates about your campaign or enquiry

We will not use your information for marketing emails you didn't ask for, and we will not pass it to anyone outside the partnership you contacted us about without telling you first.

Where your data is stored

Form submissions are processed and delivered to us by Formspree, which forwards them to our email inbox. Email communication is handled by standard email providers. We rely on these providers' own security and privacy practices to keep your data safe in transit and at rest.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data for as long as we're working together, plus a reasonable period afterwards for our own records (typically up to 24 months). If your enquiry doesn't lead to a partnership, we will delete or archive it on request.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Tell you what information we hold about you
  • Correct anything that's wrong
  • Delete your information from our records
  • Stop using your information for any specific purpose

Just email us using the address below and we'll handle it.

Third-party links

This site may link to creator channels, brand websites, or social platforms. Once you click through, you're on their site, under their privacy terms, not ours.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and refresh the "last updated" date at the top.
