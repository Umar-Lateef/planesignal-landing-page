How it works

The same process
on every deal.

Transparent, repeatable, and professional. Every time.

01
Identify the match
We find brands actively spending on sponsorships and identify creators whose audience is the right fit. Both sides are vetted before any outreach begins.
02
Approach both sides
We contact the brand and the creator separately, present the opportunity, and manage all communication. Neither party has to chase anyone.
03
Negotiate the deal
We agree on rates, deliverables, timelines, and usage rights. Both sides know exactly what they're committing to before anything is signed.
04
Manage delivery
We oversee content creation, handle the approval process, and make sure the brand receives what they paid for, on time.
05
Close and confirm
Once the content goes live, we handle the close and confirm delivery. Every deal ends with both sides satisfied and everything accounted for.
